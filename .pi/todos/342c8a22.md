Implemented `src/minidic/vad.py`:

- `VADProcessor` wraps Silero VAD with `VADIterator`
- `process(chunk_int16)` returns `(SpeechEvent.START, None)`, `(SpeechEvent.END, audio_f32)`, or `(None, None)`
- `SpeechSegmentBuffer` accumulates float32 chunks during speech, returns concatenated array on END
- Configurable: threshold (0.5), min_silence_duration_ms (300), speech_pad_ms (30)
- `.in_speech` property, `.reset()` method
- Tested: buffer concat, silence processing, reset
